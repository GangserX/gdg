Why You Can't Miss GCCD Kolkata '25

This year's event will feature hands-on workshops, technical deep dives, and success stories from industry experts who have leveraged Google Cloud to transform their businesses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who should attend GCCD Kolkata '25?

GCCD Kolkata is perfect for developers, IT professionals, cloud architects, data scientists, students, and anyone interested in cloud computing and Google technologies.

Do I need prior experience with Google Cloud?

Not at all! We have sessions for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. There will be introductory workshops for those new to Google Cloud.

Will there be opportunities for networking?

Absolutely! GCCD Kolkata '25 includes dedicated networking sessions, a networking lunch, and an evening mixer to connect with peers, speakers, and Google experts.

Are there any student discounts available?

Yes, we offer a 50% discount for students with a valid ID. Please contact us with proof of your student status to receive the discount code.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Select sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees after the event. However, workshops and hands-on sessions will not be recorded.
